A Little About Me

Hello, my name is Tyson Elfors. I am a current web development/design student at Utah Valley University. I have a passion for coding as well as creating beautiful websites. The passion that I have helps me strive to write modern, clean, effecient code. Learning modern design principles has helped me better integrate clean code with a clean UI.

The web developement and design program at UVU has been the perfect mix of writing code while still expressing my design abilities. I first started taking classes in computer science and IT fields but I wasn't passionate enough about these. That's when I found the digital media department where it was the perfect mix of code and design for me.

My education goals are to continue to learn new technologies here at UVU. I have had a lot of focus on creating stunning front end designs. In order to acheive a full stack status I am wanting and going to be learning more about the backend.

My short term career goal is to start out as a jr. developer where I can work with people to help faciliate my learning and push me to grow as a programmer. Long term I would like to become a freelance developer and provide clients with websites that will grow their business.
